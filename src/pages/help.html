IITJ AUTO FEEDBACK FILLER

What is this extension?

This extension is developed to automatically fill the feedback form of IITJ. It is a chrome extension which is available on chrome web store.

How to use this extension?

It is very easy to use this extension. Just follow the steps given below:

  • First of all, you need to install this extension from here.
  • After installing the extension, you need to login to your IITJ account.
  • Now, you need to go to the feedback form of your course.
  • After that, you need to click on the extension icon and click on the Fill Feedback button.
  • Now, you need to wait for a few seconds and the extension will automatically fill the feedback form.
  • After that, you need to click on the Submit button.
  • That's it. You have successfully filled the feedback form.
